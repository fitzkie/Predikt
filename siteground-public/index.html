FAQ

A cleaner split between the brand site and the trading apps

Why split Sports and Predikts into separate app surfaces?

Because the browsing behavior is different. Sports traders want rapid event scanning. Prediction users want thematic discovery and context.

Do both apps still use Azuro?

Yes. The frontends can diverge while sharing the same wallet integration, market infrastructure, and on-chain settlement model.

Where do the buttons go?

`Sports` points to bet.prediktmarkets.com and `Predikts` points to app.prediktmarkets.com.
