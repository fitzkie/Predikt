FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do I start using Predikt?

Open the Sports or Predikts app, connect your wallet, choose a market, and confirm your position directly from your wallet.

Why use an on-chain market platform instead of a traditional one?

Predikt keeps settlement logic on-chain, gives you wallet-native access, and removes the usual custodial deposit and withdrawal model.

Which networks and assets does the platform support?

The product is designed for EVM-based access and wallet-connected usage, with chain support and market coverage expanding through the underlying Azuro stack.

How can I trust market outcomes?

Market resolution depends on predefined conditions and verified settlement logic, so outcomes are not manually adjusted after the fact.

What happens if an event is canceled or unresolved?

Canceled, voided, or unresolved events follow the applicable market rules for settlement, which is why resolution criteria and event status matter before you trade.

Are there any geographic restrictions?

Availability depends on your jurisdiction. You should confirm whether access to sports betting or prediction markets is permitted where you are located.
